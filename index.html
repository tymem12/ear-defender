Welcome to EarDefender

Your tool to detect deepfake audio and stay informed in a digital world.

What is EarDefender?

EarDefender helps you analyze and verify audio files for authenticity. Whether you are a journalist, researcher, or a curious user, our tool provides insights into the trustworthiness of audio content online.

Why We Built It

In a world where misinformation spreads quickly, audio deepfakes are a growing concern. We created EarDefender to empower individuals with the tools needed to discern real from fake and make better-informed decisions.
